Our Story

Discover the passion, craftsmanship, and dedication behind every pair of MIAASA footwear

Our Heritage

Founded in 2010, MIAASA emerged from a simple vision: to create footwear that seamlessly blends timeless elegance with contemporary comfort. Our journey began in a small workshop in Milan, where our founder, Alessandro Moretti, combined traditional Italian shoemaking techniques with innovative design concepts.

Today, MIAASA stands as a testament to this vision, with our collections gracing the feet of discerning customers across 15 countries worldwide. Each pair tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship, premium materials, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

We believe that exceptional footwear should not only elevate your style but also provide unparalleled comfort from the first wear to the last step.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

Every MIAASA shoe is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who have honed their craft over decades. We honor traditional techniques while embracing innovation.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible sourcing and ethical production. Our leathers are sustainably sourced, and we continuously work to reduce our environmental footprint.

Quality

From material selection to final inspection, we maintain uncompromising standards. Each pair undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure lasting durability and comfort.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

MIAASA was founded in Milan with a small collection of handcrafted leather shoes. Our first workshop opened its doors with just three master craftsmen.

2013

First International Recognition

Our Heritage Collection received the prestigious Italian Design Award, marking our entry into the international luxury footwear market.

2016

Global Expansion

We opened flagship stores in Paris, London, and New York, establishing MIAASA as a global brand while maintaining our artisanal roots.

2019

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our comprehensive sustainability program, committing to ethically sourced materials and carbon-neutral production by 2025.

2023

Digital Innovation

Introduced our virtual fitting technology and expanded our e-commerce presence, making MIAASA accessible to customers worldwide.
