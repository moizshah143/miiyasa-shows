Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items in their original packaging with the original receipt. Items must be in resalable condition. Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.

We provide detailed size guides for each product on our website. If you need further assistance, our customer service team can help you find the perfect fit. You can also visit our physical store for a fitting.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can calculate shipping costs during checkout by entering your destination country.

Each pair comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend using a soft brush to remove dirt, storing in a cool dry place, and using appropriate cleaners for the specific material. Avoid exposing leather shoes to excessive moisture.

We offer limited customization options on select styles. Please contact our customer service team to inquire about customization possibilities for specific products. Lead times for customized orders are typically 3-4 weeks.
